## 2026-07-04 - Securing Sidebar State Cookie\n**Vulnerability:** The sidebar state cookie was being set without the `Secure` flag.\n**Learning:** While not strictly sensitive data, setting the `Secure` flag on all cookies is a good defense-in-depth habit to ensure cookies are only transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections, preventing Man-in-the-Middle (MitM) attacks.\n**Prevention:** When setting `document.cookie`, always include the `Secure` attribute in addition to `SameSite` and `max-age` settings, even for seemingly innocuous UI state data.
